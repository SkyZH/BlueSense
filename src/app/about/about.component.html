About

Data are collected from Arduino at SkyZH's home.

Sensors & Board:

  • Raspberry Pi 3 Model B
  • Serial via
  • DFRobot Bluno Mega 2560
  • Serial & I2C
  • Gravity: I2C BME280 Environmental Sensor (Temperature, Humidity, Barometer)
  • PM2.5 Laser Dust Sensor

Data are refreshed every minute and are displayed realtime.

BlueSense is built with Firebase and Angular.
